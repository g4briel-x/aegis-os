Aegis OS — AI Cost and Latency Policy

Version: 0.1
Status: AI Policy Document

1. Introduction

This document defines how Aegis OS balances quality, speed and cost across model execution.

2. Policy Dimensions

The policy should consider:

reasoning quality;
response time;
token cost;
model availability;
task criticality.
3. Policy Modes

Possible modes:

premium-quality;
low-latency;
budget-aware;
enterprise-private;
balanced-default.
4. Final Principle

Cost and latency should be explicit policy choices, not accidental side effects of model selection.
