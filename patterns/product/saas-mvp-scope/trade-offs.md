# SaaS MVP Scope — Trade-Offs

Version: 0.1.0  
Status: Draft

---

# 1. Benefits

This Pattern improves:

```text
delivery speed
focus
customer learning
engineering clarity
launch readiness
funding or pilot validation
roadmap discipline
```

---

# 2. Costs

This Pattern may reduce:

```text
feature breadth
enterprise readiness
automation depth
customization
market coverage
visual polish
advanced reporting
```

---

# 3. Product Trade-Offs

A focused MVP may disappoint users outside the target segment.

Mitigation:

```text
state target audience clearly
capture requests for later
avoid promising broad platform features
prioritize early segment fit
```

---

# 4. Engineering Trade-Offs

A fast MVP may use simpler technical choices.

Mitigation:

```text
keep architecture modular
avoid irreversible shortcuts
document manual operations
protect data boundaries
use migration-ready schema
```

---

# 5. Business Trade-Offs

A narrow MVP may limit early revenue opportunities.

Mitigation:

```text
choose a segment with strong pain
use paid pilots
validate willingness to pay
expand after proof
```

---

# 6. Main Risks

Key risks:

```text
MVP too small to deliver value
MVP too broad to ship fast
manual process becomes permanent
wrong target segment selected
success criteria missing
non-scope ignored under pressure
```

---

# 7. Mitigations

Mitigate with:

- customer discovery;
- scope scoring;
- non-scope list;
- weekly scope review;
- activation metric;
- pilot feedback;
- launch readiness checklist.

---

# 8. Final Principle

> MVP trade-offs are acceptable only when they increase learning speed without destroying user trust.
