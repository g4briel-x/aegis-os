# 1. Purpose

The Create Go-To-Market Plan Playbook provides a structured procedure for defining how a SaaS product reaches, converts and retains its first customers.

It helps teams align market segment, positioning, offer, channels, acquisition strategy, sales motion, launch plan, metrics and learning cadence.

---

# 2. Trigger

Use this Playbook when:

- a SaaS product is preparing for launch;
- an MVP needs its first customers;
- a product has pricing but no acquisition plan;
- a founder needs a clear sales and marketing path;
- a team needs to choose launch channels;
- a pilot or beta program must be filled;
- revenue growth needs structured execution.

---

# 3. Scope

This Playbook covers:

- target market definition;
- customer segment selection;
- positioning;
- offer design;
- channel strategy;
- sales motion;
- launch campaign planning;
- funnel metrics;
- learning cadence;
- GTM risk review.

This Playbook does not replace full brand strategy, but it creates a practical execution plan for reaching the market.

# 5. Final Principle

> A go-to-market plan is strong when it defines who to reach, why they care, how to reach them and how success will be measured.
