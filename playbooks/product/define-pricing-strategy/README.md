---

# 1. Purpose

The Define Pricing Strategy Playbook provides a structured procedure for designing SaaS pricing that connects customer value, packaging, willingness to pay, unit economics and go-to-market motion.

It helps teams move from vague pricing ideas to clear plans, tiers, metrics, assumptions, risks and validation experiments.

---

# 2. Trigger

Use this Playbook when:

- a SaaS product needs initial pricing;
- an MVP is preparing for launch;
- existing pricing is unclear or ineffective;
- customer segments have different value levels;
- packaging must be defined;
- subscription tiers need structure;
- pricing must support revenue and growth goals;
- a founder or product team needs a monetization model.

---

# 3. Scope

This Playbook covers:

- customer segmentation;
- value driver analysis;
- pricing metric selection;
- packaging and tiering;
- free trial or freemium review;
- willingness-to-pay validation;
- competitor reference review;
- unit economics check;
- pricing risks;
- pricing experiment plan.

This Playbook does not replace financial modeling, but it creates the product and business logic for pricing decisions.

# 5. Final Principle

> SaaS pricing should charge for customer value in a way that is simple to understand, profitable to deliver and testable in the market.
