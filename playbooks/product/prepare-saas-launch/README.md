---

# 1. Purpose

The Prepare SaaS Launch Playbook provides a structured procedure for preparing a SaaS product, MVP or major feature for market launch.

It helps teams align product readiness, positioning, onboarding, pricing, support, analytics, launch communication, risk management and post-launch learning before opening access to users or customers.

---

# 2. Trigger

Use this Playbook when:

- a SaaS MVP is close to launch;
- a beta, pilot or public launch is planned;
- a major product feature needs launch preparation;
- marketing, product and engineering need launch alignment;
- onboarding and support must be prepared;
- pricing and packaging must be communicated;
- post-launch metrics need to be tracked.

---

# 3. Scope

This Playbook covers:

- launch goals;
- target audience;
- product readiness;
- pricing readiness;
- onboarding readiness;
- support readiness;
- analytics readiness;
- launch messaging;
- risk review;
- launch checklist;
- post-launch monitoring and learning.

This Playbook does not replace go-to-market strategy. It focuses on launch execution readiness.

# 5. Final Principle

> A SaaS launch is ready when the product works, the offer is clear, users can start successfully and the team knows how to learn from the first market signals.
