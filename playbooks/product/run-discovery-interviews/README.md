Version: 0.1.0  
Status: Premium Draft  
Domain: Product  
Category: Product Discovery

---

# 1. Purpose

The Run Discovery Interviews Playbook provides a structured procedure for preparing, conducting, analyzing and converting customer discovery interviews into product decisions.

It helps validate problems, understand user workflows, identify pain intensity, detect buying signals and reduce product risk before building.

---

# 2. Trigger

Use this Playbook when:

- a product idea needs validation;
- a SaaS MVP needs evidence before build;
- customer pain is unclear;
- user segments need clarification;
- pricing or willingness to pay needs exploration;
- a feature request needs deeper understanding;
- a team needs qualitative evidence for roadmap decisions.

---

# 3. Scope

This Playbook covers:

- discovery objective definition;
- participant targeting;
- interview script preparation;
- question design;
- interview execution;
- evidence capture;
- insight synthesis;
- decision recommendation.

This Playbook does not replace quantitative research, but it provides the qualitative foundation for stronger product decisions.
