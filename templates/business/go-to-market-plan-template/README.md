# 1. Purpose

The Go-To-Market Plan Template provides a reusable structure for planning how a SaaS product, MVP, feature, pilot or launch will reach the right customers and convert them into users or buyers.

It helps product, marketing, sales, business and leadership teams align on target segment, ICP, positioning, messaging, channels, launch motion, sales process, pricing, metrics, risks and execution ownership.

---

# 2. When to Use

Use this Template when:

```text
a SaaS MVP is preparing for launch
a new product is entering a market
a paid pilot must be planned
a feature needs market communication
a launch campaign is required
sales and marketing need alignment
positioning and ICP must be clarified
```
