# Go-To-Market Plan Template — Usage Guide

Version: 0.1.0  
Status: Draft

---

# 1. Usage Process

Use this sequence:

```text
1. Define product or offer
2. Define target segment and ICP
3. Clarify customer problem and urgency
4. Write value proposition and positioning
5. Define offer and call to action
6. Select channels
7. Define sales motion
8. Map funnel and metrics
9. Plan launch assets and execution
10. Review risks, assumptions and owners
```

---

# 2. Recommended Review Flow

Review in this order:

```text
Product review
Business review
Marketing review
Sales review
Customer support review if customer-facing
Leadership approval for major launch
```

---

# 3. Writing Rules

A GTM Plan should:

- focus on one primary segment;
- use specific customer language;
- avoid vague positioning;
- define a concrete offer;
- choose channels deliberately;
- assign owners;
- define measurable success;
- document assumptions.

---

# 4. Positioning Rule

Weak:

```text
The best platform for creators.
```

Better:

```text
A readiness platform that helps independent producers prepare financing-ready project packages before investor conversations.
```

---

# 5. Channel Rule

Do not list every channel. Choose channels that fit the ICP and available execution capacity.

---

# 6. Final Principle

> Use the GTM Plan Template to focus launch energy instead of spreading effort across vague audiences and channels.
