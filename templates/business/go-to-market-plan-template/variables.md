# 3. Variable Descriptions

## `{{product_or_offer}}`

Purpose:

```text
Product, MVP, feature, pilot or offer being launched.
```

Example:

```text
Project Readiness Review SaaS
```

---

## `{{primary_segment}}`

Purpose:

```text
Initial market segment targeted by the launch.
```

Example:

```text
Independent film producers preparing financing-ready project packages.
```

---

## `{{value_proposition}}`

Purpose:

```text
Main promise of value to the target segment.
```

Example:

```text
Helps producers identify missing financing materials before approaching investors.
```

---

## `{{primary_channel_1}}`

Purpose:

```text
Main acquisition or distribution channel.
```

Example:

```text
Founder-led outreach to independent producer communities.
```

---

## `{{primary_launch_metric}}`

Purpose:

```text
Metric used to evaluate launch success.
```

Example:

```text
20 qualified project submissions during the pilot month.
```

---

# 4. Final Principle

> GTM variables should make the market, message, offer and metric explicit.
