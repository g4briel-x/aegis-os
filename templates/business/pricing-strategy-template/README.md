# 1. Purpose

The Pricing Strategy Template provides a reusable structure for defining SaaS pricing strategy, pricing tiers, value metrics, packaging, trial rules, paid pilots, discounts, upgrade paths and validation experiments.

It helps product, business, sales, finance and engineering teams connect customer value, willingness to pay, product entitlements and revenue goals.

---

# 2. When to Use

Use this Template when:

```text
a SaaS product needs pricing
a paid pilot must be structured
subscription tiers are being defined
feature packaging must be clarified
pricing assumptions need validation
upgrade and downgrade logic must be planned
the product needs a monetization model before launch
```

This Template produces:

