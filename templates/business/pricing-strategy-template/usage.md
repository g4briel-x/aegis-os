# Pricing Strategy Template — Usage Guide

Version: 0.1.0  
Status: Draft

---

# 1. Usage Process

Use this sequence:

```text
1. Define target segment and buyer
2. Clarify customer value and urgency
3. Choose pricing objective
4. Select pricing model
5. Define value metric
6. Build plan structure
7. Map entitlements to plans
8. Define trial, pilot or freemium entry
9. Validate willingness to pay
10. Prepare billing implementation requirements
```

---

# 2. Recommended Review Flow

Review in this order:

```text
Product review
Business review
Sales review
Finance review if applicable
Engineering review for billing feasibility
Leadership approval for launch pricing
```

---

# 3. Writing Rules

A Pricing Strategy should:

- focus on customer value;
- avoid too many tiers;
- define what changes between plans;
- connect pricing to entitlements;
- include validation assumptions;
- avoid copying competitors blindly;
- identify billing implementation needs.

---

# 4. Pricing Rule

Weak:

```text
Price low to attract users.
```

Better:

```text
Use a paid pilot price that filters serious creators while validating whether the readiness review produces enough value to support subscription pricing.
```

---

# 5. Final Principle

> Use the Pricing Strategy Template to validate the business model before hardcoding billing behavior.
